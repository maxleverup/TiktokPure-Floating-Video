📱 Mobile View — How to use
Float TikTok in mobile view on any tab.
Enable the feature then open any tab — the TikTok panel will appear on the right side of your screen.
Action Bar (bottom of panel):
  • 🔇 Mute / Unmute — Toggle sound for the TikTok panel
  • 🔒 Lock / Locked — Lock the aspect ratio when resizing
  • ✕ Close — Hide the panel (TikTok keeps running in background)
⌨️ Shortcut: Press F8 to quickly show/hide the panel on any tab.
Note: F8 only works when Mobile View is enabled in settings.
